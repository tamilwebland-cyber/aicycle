Powerful AI Prompts for Better Results

Discover ready-to-use prompts for ChatGPT and other AI assistants. Copy a prompt, customize it for your needs, and get started.

Popular AI Prompts

✍️ Blog Post Generator

Use this prompt to create a detailed and well-structured blog article.

Write a detailed and engaging blog post about [TOPIC] for [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Use a natural, human-friendly tone. Include a compelling introduction, clear headings and subheadings, practical examples, key takeaways, and an FAQ section. Make the article informative and easy to understand.

💼 Business Idea Generator

Generate practical business ideas based on your interests and budget.

Act as a business consultant. Suggest 10 practical business ideas for someone interested in [INDUSTRY] with a starting budget of [BUDGET] in [LOCATION]. For each idea, explain the target customers, how the business can make money, estimated startup requirements, and the first three steps to get started.

📢 Social Media Content Creator

Create engaging social media content for your business or brand.

Act as a social media marketing expert. Create 10 engaging social media post ideas for a [BUSINESS TYPE] targeting [TARGET AUDIENCE]. For each idea, provide a short caption, a call to action, and relevant hashtags. Keep the tone natural and avoid exaggerated marketing claims.

💻 Coding Assistant

Get help understanding and improving your code.

Act as an experienced software developer. Review the following [PROGRAMMING LANGUAGE] code. Identify bugs, security concerns, and performance issues. Explain each problem in simple language and provide an improved version of the code. Do not change functionality unless necessary. [PASTE YOUR CODE HERE]

✍️ Rewrite Text Naturally

Rewrite any text in a clear and natural style.

Rewrite the following text so it sounds natural, clear, and genuinely human-written. Keep the original meaning, but improve the flow, sentence structure, and readability. Avoid unnecessary jargon, repetitive phrases, and exaggerated language. [PASTE YOUR TEXT HERE]

✍️ Improve My Writing

Improve grammar, clarity, and readability without changing your message.

Act as an experienced editor. Improve the following text for grammar, spelling, clarity, and readability. Preserve my original meaning and tone. Do not add information that is not present in the original text. [PASTE YOUR TEXT HERE]

💼 Business Plan Creator

Turn a business idea into a practical step-by-step plan.

Act as a small business consultant. Create a practical business plan for [BUSINESS IDEA] in [LOCATION] with a starting budget of [BUDGET]. Include target customers, products or services, startup requirements, expected expenses, pricing strategy, marketing ideas, possible risks, and a 30-day action plan.

💼 Competitor Analysis

Build a framework for comparing your business with competitors.

Act as a business strategy consultant. Help me analyze competitors in the [INDUSTRY] market in [LOCATION]. Create a competitor analysis framework covering target customers, products or services, pricing, strengths, weaknesses, marketing channels, customer experience, and possible opportunities for differentiation. Clearly identify any information that requires current market research.

📢 Facebook Post Creator

Create a natural Facebook post for your business, website, or project.

Write a natural and engaging Facebook post about [TOPIC OR PRODUCT] for [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Start with an interesting opening, explain the value clearly, and finish with a simple call to action. Avoid sounding overly promotional. Include up to 5 relevant hashtags.

📢 Instagram Caption Generator

Create an engaging caption that matches your post.

Create an Instagram caption for a post about [TOPIC]. The target audience is [TARGET AUDIENCE]. Use a natural and engaging tone, a strong opening line, short readable paragraphs, and a clear call to action. Suggest up to 10 relevant hashtags without using unrelated trending hashtags.

💻 Explain Code Simply

Understand complicated code in beginner-friendly language.

Act as a patient programming teacher. Explain the following [PROGRAMMING LANGUAGE] code to a beginner. Describe what the code does, explain the important parts step by step, and point out any potential problems. Use simple language and practical examples where helpful. [PASTE YOUR CODE HERE]

🎓 Personal Study Plan

Create a realistic learning schedule for any subject.

Act as a study planner. Create a [NUMBER OF DAYS/WEEKS] study plan to help me learn [SUBJECT OR SKILL]. My current level is [BEGINNER/INTERMEDIATE/ADVANCED], and I can study for [TIME] each day. Break the plan into daily or weekly goals, practice activities, revision sessions, and progress checks.

📈 Daily Productivity Planner

Organize your tasks into a realistic daily schedule.

Act as a productivity assistant. Organize the following tasks into a realistic plan for my day. Prioritize important and time-sensitive tasks, include reasonable breaks, and avoid creating an overloaded schedule. Explain which tasks should be completed first and why. My available time: [AVAILABLE TIME] My tasks: [PASTE YOUR TASKS HERE]

📹 YouTube Video Idea Generator

Generate relevant video ideas for your YouTube niche.

Act as a YouTube content strategist. Suggest 10 useful video ideas for a channel about [NICHE] targeting [TARGET AUDIENCE]. For each idea, provide a clickable but non-misleading title, a one-sentence video concept, and explain why viewers in this niche may find the topic useful or interesting.

📧 Professional Email Writer

Create clear and professional emails for any situation.

Write a professional email about [TOPIC OR PURPOSE] to [RECIPIENT]. My goal is [GOAL]. Use a polite, clear, and natural tone. Include an appropriate subject line, keep the email concise, and end with a suitable call to action or closing.

📄 Resume/CV Improvement Assistant

Improve your resume to make your skills and experience clearer.

Act as an experienced resume editor. Review my resume for a [JOB TITLE] position. Improve the clarity, structure, and wording while keeping all information accurate. Highlight relevant skills and achievements without inventing experience or qualifications. Suggest specific improvements and provide revised wording where useful. [PASTE YOUR RESUME HERE]

🎯 Job Interview Preparation Coach

Prepare for interviews with relevant questions and practice answers.

Act as a job interview coach. I am preparing for a [JOB TITLE] interview in the [INDUSTRY] industry. Generate 10 likely interview questions, explain what the interviewer may be looking for in each answer, and help me structure strong responses based on my experience. Include 3 thoughtful questions I can ask the interviewer.

🔍 SEO Keyword & Content Idea Generator

Generate SEO topic and keyword ideas for your website.

Act as an SEO content strategist. Generate keyword and content ideas for a website about [NICHE] targeting people in [LOCATION OR AUDIENCE]. Organize the ideas by search intent: informational, commercial, and transactional. Suggest useful long-tail keyword ideas and related article topics. Clearly note that actual search volume and competition should be verified with current SEO data.

🛒 Product Description Writer

Create useful and persuasive product descriptions.

Write a clear and engaging product description for [PRODUCT NAME]. The target customer is [TARGET CUSTOMER]. Highlight the product's key features and explain their practical benefits. Use a natural tone and avoid exaggerated or unsupported claims. Include a short description, key benefits, and a simple call to action. Product details: [PASTE PRODUCT DETAILS HERE]

💰 Personal Budget Planner

Organize income and expenses into a practical monthly budget.

Act as a budgeting assistant. Help me create a practical monthly budget based on the information below. Organize expenses into essential spending, discretionary spending, savings, and financial goals. Identify areas where I may be able to reduce spending without making unrealistic assumptions. Monthly income: [INCOME] Fixed expenses: [FIXED EXPENSES] Other expenses: [OTHER EXPENSES] Savings goal: [SAVINGS GOAL]

✈️ Travel Itinerary Planner

Create a personalized travel plan for your next trip.

Act as a travel planner. Create a [NUMBER OF DAYS]-day itinerary for a trip to [DESTINATION] for [NUMBER OF TRAVELERS]. Our approximate budget is [BUDGET], and we are interested in [INTERESTS]. Suggest a balanced daily schedule with attractions, meal breaks, travel time, and rest. Mention which details, such as opening hours and ticket prices, should be checked before the trip.

🗣️ Language Learning Practice Partner

Practice conversations and improve your language skills.

Act as a friendly language tutor. Help me practice [LANGUAGE]. My current level is [BEGINNER/INTERMEDIATE/ADVANCED]. Start a realistic conversation about [TOPIC]. Ask me one question at a time and wait for my response. Correct important grammar or vocabulary mistakes politely, explain the correction simply, and suggest a more natural way to express my answer.

📊 Data Analysis Assistant

Analyze data and identify useful patterns and insights.

Act as a data analyst. Analyze the data I provide and summarize the most important patterns, trends, unusual values, and possible insights. Explain your reasoning clearly and distinguish between observations supported by the data and assumptions that require further investigation. Suggest useful calculations or visualizations that could improve the analysis. [PASTE YOUR DATA OR DATA DESCRIPTION HERE]

🎨 AI Image Generation Prompt Creator

Turn your visual idea into a detailed AI image prompt.

Act as an AI image prompt specialist. Turn my idea into a detailed image-generation prompt. My idea: [DESCRIBE YOUR IMAGE] Include the main subject, environment, composition, perspective, lighting, mood, important visual details, and preferred aspect ratio. Keep the description clear and coherent. Do not add copyrighted characters, logos, or real people unless I specifically request them.

📝 Meeting Notes Summarizer

Turn lengthy meeting notes into a clear and useful summary.

Act as a professional meeting assistant. Review the meeting notes below and create a concise summary. Organize the output into key discussion points, decisions made, action items, responsible people (if mentioned), and deadlines. Do not invent information that is not present in the notes. [PASTE MEETING NOTES HERE]

💡 Brainstorming & Idea Generator

Generate fresh ideas and explore different possibilities.

Act as a creative brainstorming partner. Generate 15 practical and original ideas related to [TOPIC OR PROBLEM]. Include a mix of simple, unconventional, and ambitious ideas. For each idea, provide a short explanation of how it could work. Avoid repeating similar concepts and highlight the 3 ideas that appear most practical based on the information provided.

📑 Presentation Outline Creator

Create a clear structure for a professional presentation.

Act as a presentation specialist. Create a structured presentation outline about [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE]. The presentation should last approximately [DURATION]. Suggest a title and organize the content slide by slide. For each slide, provide a heading, key talking points, and a suggestion for a useful visual or chart where appropriate. Finish with a clear conclusion and next steps.

🛍️ E-commerce Store Name Generator

Generate memorable name ideas for an online store.

Act as a brand naming specialist. Suggest 20 memorable name ideas for an e-commerce store selling [PRODUCT TYPE] to [TARGET AUDIENCE]. The brand personality should feel [BRAND STYLE]. Keep the names easy to pronounce and remember. Briefly explain the idea behind each name. Remind me to verify domain, trademark, and social media availability before choosing a name.

🤝 Customer Service Response Writer

Create polite and helpful responses to customer messages.

Act as an experienced customer service representative. Write a professional and empathetic response to the customer message below. Acknowledge the customer's concern, explain any relevant information clearly, and suggest an appropriate next step. Do not promise refunds, compensation, or outcomes unless I specifically provide that policy. Customer message: [PASTE CUSTOMER MESSAGE HERE] Relevant company policy or information: [PASTE INFORMATION HERE]

📚 Complex Topic Simplifier

Understand difficult subjects through simple explanations.

Explain [COMPLEX TOPIC] in simple language for someone with no previous knowledge of the subject. Start with a short overview, then explain the main concepts step by step. Use an everyday analogy where helpful and define any technical terms you need to use. Finish with 5 key points I should remember.

🧠 Decision-Making Assistant

Compare your options using a structured decision process.

Act as a neutral decision-making assistant. Help me choose between the following options: [LIST YOUR OPTIONS] My main goal is: [GOAL] My priorities are: [PRIORITIES] My constraints are: [BUDGET, TIME, LOCATION, OR OTHER LIMITS] Compare each option based on my priorities. Explain the advantages, disadvantages, risks, and trade-offs. Do not make the final decision for me. Instead, identify which option appears strongest under different priorities and explain why.

📰 News Article Summarizer & Fact-Check Guide

Understand a news story and identify claims that need verification.

Analyze the news article or text below. Provide a neutral summary of the main points and separate factual claims from opinions or predictions. Identify important claims that should be independently verified and suggest what types of reliable primary or reputable sources could be used to check them. Do not claim that something is true or false unless sufficient evidence is provided. [PASTE NEWS ARTICLE OR TEXT HERE]

🏗️ Project Planning Assistant

Turn a project idea into clear tasks and milestones.

Act as a project planning assistant. Create a practical project plan for [PROJECT NAME OR GOAL]. The target completion date is [DATE OR TIMEFRAME], and the available resources are [RESOURCES]. Break the project into phases, milestones, and individual tasks. Identify dependencies, possible risks, and priorities. Finish with a simple checklist of the first 5 actions to take.

📱 Mobile App Idea Generator

Discover practical app ideas based on real user problems.

Act as a mobile product strategist. Generate 10 mobile app ideas for [TARGET AUDIENCE OR INDUSTRY]. Focus on solving genuine user problems rather than copying popular apps. For each idea, explain the problem it solves, target users, core features, possible monetization options, and the simplest MVP version that could be built first. Highlight any idea that would require significant legal, privacy, or regulatory considerations.
