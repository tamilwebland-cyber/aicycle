Google Gemini

Google's AI assistant for productivity, coding and research.

Official Website

Overview

Google Gemini is a multimodal AI assistant that can understand text, images and other inputs. It integrates with many Google services.

Top Features

  • Text and image understanding
  • Writing and brainstorming
  • Coding assistance
  • Google Workspace integration
  • Research support
  • Multilingual conversations
